Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [To be filled in before launch]

The ZORA Team ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ZORA mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use ZORA.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address if you sign up for launch notifications.
  • Health data: Medication names, dosages, schedules, and any notes you enter into the App. This data is stored on your device and, if you enable sync, in your personal iCloud account.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about how features are used, to help us improve the App. This data does not include your health information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's functionality.
  • To send you launch notifications if you opted in on our website.
  • To understand how users interact with ZORA at an aggregate level.

We do not sell your personal information or health data to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your health data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, data is stored in your personal iCloud account and governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect information we do store (such as email addresses from notification sign-ups).

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics (e.g., aggregate usage statistics). These services do not receive your health data. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal information we hold about you (such as your email address) at any time by contacting us at [your email here]. Health data stored on your device can be deleted by removing the App.

6. Children's Privacy

ZORA is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [your email here].
